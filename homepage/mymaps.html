Maps(current) About Canarsie Essay

Below this text is the map of Brooklyn, which is sourced from Google Maps, and its neighborhoods. If you press Ctrl and scroll, you can see the most important places in the different neighborhoods. The red line represents the outline of Brooklyn, which serves to differentiate Brooklyn from Queens. In the essay, I will be talking about Canarsie and Sheepshead Bay, so it is important to know where they are located. Sheepshead Bay is a neighborhood located in the southern part of Brooklyn, and the neighborhood that is closest to it is Gravesend. A major attraction in Sheepshead Bay is Marine Park, which is a public park with an acreage of about 798 acres. Canarsie is located in the eastern part of Brooklyn.
